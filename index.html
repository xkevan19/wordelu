How to Play & Score

1. Getting Started

•Your Name: Enter a name for the Leaderboard (optional for guests, automatic if logged in).
•
Difficulty:
◦Easy: No time limit.
◦Hard: 120 seconds (2 minutes) to guess!
•Category: Choose a word theme or keep it General.
•Click Start Game!

2. Gameplay

•You have 6 attempts to guess the secret 5-letter word.
•Type your guess using your keyboard or the on-screen keyboard.
•Press Enter to submit.

3. Color Coding

Green: Correct letter, correct position.
Yellow: Correct letter, wrong position.
Gray: Letter not in the word.

The on-screen keyboard updates with these colors too!

4. Winning & Losing

•Win: Guess the word within 6 tries (and time if on Hard).
•Lose: Run out of attempts or time (on Hard).

5. Points System

•
Points per Guess:
◦+2 points per Green letter.
◦+1 point per Yellow letter.
◦0 points for Gray letters.
•
First Try Bonus: +20 points total (instead of per-letter points) for guessing on the first try.
•
Hard Mode Time Bonus (on Win): +1 point for every 4 seconds remaining.
•
Cumulative Score (Logged-in Users): Your score from each winning game is added to your total score on the Leaderboard.
•
Guest Scores: Displayed per-game and on a temporary local leaderboard.

6. Extra Features

•Use the menu buttons to check the Leaderboard (shows logged-in users or local guests), Team Standings (logged-in only), your Statistics (logged-in only), and unlocked Achievements (logged-in only).
•Log in or Sign up to track cumulative stats and achievements.
•Logged-in users can manage their Account details.
